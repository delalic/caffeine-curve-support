# Caffeine Curve: Support

Welcome to the Support Center for **Caffeine Curve**. We are here to help you track your caffeine intake safely and effectively.

---

### ☕ How to Get Help
If you are experiencing technical issues, have questions about the app's features, or would like to suggest a new functionality, please reach out to us directly.

**Support Email:** caffeine-curve-support@delalic.com
---

### 🛠️ Common Troubleshooting
If the app isn't behaving as expected, please try these quick steps before emailing:
1. **Check for Updates:** Ensure you are running the latest version of **Caffeine Curve** from the App Store.
2. **Restart the App:** Force-close and reopen the app to refresh its local state.
3. **Check iOS Version:** Ensure your device is running a compatible version of iOS.

---

### 📩 What to Include in Your Support Email
To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:
* A brief description of the problem.
* Your device model (e.g., iPhone 17 Pro Max).
* Your current iOS version.
* Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable).
